SERVICES

What we can help you with

We provide manual QA and light development for realistic apps and sites. Bring your own product or ask us to build and test a small demo end-to-end.

Mobile apps

Mobile app testing

Manual testing on Android (real devices and emulators), basic iOS simulator checks, UX verification against platform guidelines, crash reporting and log collection for your dev team.

Web & SaaS

Manual functional testing

Smoke, sanity and regression cycles for web apps, admin panels and SaaS tools. Core flows, edge cases, negative scenarios, state persistence and error handling.

Languages & locales

Visual and functional checks for English, Spanish and Cyrillic locales. Text expansion/truncation, broken layouts, mixed languages and encoding issues.

PROCESS

How we work

We keep it simple. You always know what we’re testing, how long it takes and how to read the results.

Step 1

Quick intro & scope

Short call or async messages so we understand your app, release schedule and priorities.

Step 2

Test plan & estimate

We propose a concrete test scope (what we cover), time estimate and a simple format for bug reports.

Step 3

Execution & reporting

We run the agreed tests, log issues with clear steps to reproduce and keep you updated on progress.

Step 4

Fix verification & summary

After fixes we re-test the changed areas and send a short release-ready summary for your team.
